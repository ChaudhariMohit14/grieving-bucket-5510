Overview

Get ready for an immersive experience of skiing at an off-beat destination located in the Barod village of Manali, surrounded by the giant upper Himalayan ranges. On this Mindful Retreat, unwind at the homestay located at the edge of beautiful apple orchards and overlooking the mighty Hampta Pass, offering 5 bedrooms, a small front yard, a gorgeous unrestricted terrace, and a cozy common room with plenty of beautiful unrestricted views! Hike to the picture-perfect Barod Parsha waterfall, 15 minutes from the retreat, and be greeted by the pine forests and undefined hiking trails all around. Come, and weave stories at this winter wonderland!

Book now to avail free one-time rescheduling option. Let us know 72 hours ahead of the stay and pick another date as per your convenience within the next three months.

How to reach:

    You can find ‘The Other Destination’ on google maps here.
    By Air: Book a flight for Bhuntar (Kullu), followed by a drive of about 1 hour and 10 minutes to the destination.
    By Bus: Book an overnight bus for Manali from Delhi or Chandigarh. Night buses drop off early in the morning at the Manali bus stand. Take a taxi to the property for about 500 INR.
    By Road: Enjoy a scenic drive for about 11hrs of national highways to reach Manali. Cabs are available for Chandigarh (7hrs) or Delhi (11hrs).
